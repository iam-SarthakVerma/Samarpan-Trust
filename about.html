Our Organization History

Empowering Communities, Transforming Lives

At Samarpan, we believe that meaningful change begins when people come together with a shared purpose. Our mission is to uplift underprivileged communities by creating opportunities in education, social welfare, healthcare, and community development.

  • Promote access to quality education and learning resources.
  • Encourage volunteer participation and community engagement.
  • Support social welfare initiatives for vulnerable communities.
  • Foster compassion, inclusivity, and sustainable development.

Through every campaign, donation, and volunteer effort, Samarpan remains committed to creating a positive and lasting impact. We work to bridge social gaps, empower individuals, and inspire collective action that transforms lives and strengthens communities for future generations.

Building a Future of Hope,Opportunity, and Equality

At Samarpan, we envision a society where every individual, regardless of their background or circumstances, has access to quality education, essential healthcare, and opportunities for personal growth. We strive to create communities that are empowered, self-reliant, and united by compassion and mutual support.

  • Foster a culture of kindness, responsibility, and service.
  • Promote social inclusion and community empowerment.
  • Build lasting solutions that create meaningful social impact.
  • Inspire future generations to contribute positively to society.

Through dedication, innovation, and community collaboration, Samarpan aims to become a catalyst for positive change. Our vision is a future where every person has the resources, support, and confidence needed to lead a fulfilling life and contribute to the betterment of society.

Serving Communities, Creating Impact

Samarpan was established in 2025 in Bagaha, West Champaran, Bihar (PIN Code: 845101) with a vision to create meaningful and lasting change in society. Founded on the principles of compassion, service, and community welfare, Samarpan began as a grassroots initiative dedicated to supporting individuals and families facing social and economic challenges.

  • Started with the mission of serving underprivileged communities.
  • Promoted access to education and learning opportunities for students.
  • Built a growing network of dedicated volunteers and generous donors.
  • Organized awareness campaigns and community development programs.

Since its founding in 2025 in Bagaha, West Champaran, Samarpan has worked to uplift communities through education, social welfare, and community service. Guided by compassion and collective action, we continue to create meaningful opportunities and lasting social impact.
